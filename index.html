Projects

House Sales Data Analysis Project:

Using pre-obtained data of housing sales from King County, USA, I applied various data analysis techniques to predict housing prices from various features such as square footage, number of bedrooms, etc. From this information, I was able to determine the correlation between features of houses and the effect that it had on housing market in King County.

Research Paper on the Analysis of the American Revolution:

Argumentative paper on the success of the American Revolution that led to the formation of the United States of America. The impact of Britain's control and the effect that it had on the wider spread issues within the Thirteen Colonies.
